GPS Check-in

Worker taps "Clock In" — phone grabs GPS. Admin sees a map pin. No more "yeah mate I'm on site" when they're still in bed. Geofence alerts if they're 500m off. Works offline.

v4 New
💬

Team Chat

Per-job chat threads. Worker messages "Need more cement" — admin sees it instantly. Read receipts. No emoji, no GIFs — it's a tool, not WhatsApp.

v4 New
🔄

Shift Swaps

Worker requests Friday off. System shows available coworkers. Swap accepted → admin approves → calendar updates. No more 6am phone calls.

v4 New
💰

Job Costing

Revenue £850 − Labour £320 − Materials £110 = Margin £420. Auto-calculated from GPS hours. Flag jobs bleeding money. Export to CSV for your accountant.

📅

Drag & Drop Calendar

Move jobs between workers on a week view. Drag a job card from Dave to Steve — done. No menus, no forms, just drag it.

v3
🔐

Worker PIN Login

No email, no password. Workers get a 4-digit PIN. They type it, they're in. Built for blokes who can't remember their own phone number.

OpenStreetMap

Free maps. No Google API keys. No monthly bills. Job locations on a proper map with status colours. Leaflet + OSM — open-source, forever free.

Job Status Tracking

Pending → Dispatched → In Progress → Completed → Cancelled. Every job has a status. Admin sees exactly what's happening across all sites.
